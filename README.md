# KindredCompass

Find places that feel like you.

KindredCompass is an iOS app that matches you to restaurants, activities, 
and hotels based on your personal DNA — food preferences, music taste, 
and lifestyle.

## Download
[Available on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/kindredcompass/id6743690961)

## Website
[kindredcompass.com](https://chuckg73-create.github.io)

## Features
- AI-powered DNA matching
- Restaurants, activities & hotels
- Offline favorites
- Home screen widgets
- Trip planner & RV mode
- Share spots with friends
